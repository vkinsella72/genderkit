UK helplines and advice

In immediate danger?

Phone 999. If you can't use a phone, use emergencySMS or Textphone 18000.

Police

Phone 101 to contact the police if it isn't an emergency.

Medical help

Phone 111 for medical help if it isn't an emergency.

Bullying

  • Mermaids

    Mermaids provide a helpline and email support for transgender youth.

Domestic abuse

  • National LGBT Domestic Abuse Helpline

    Galop run a helpline supporting LGBT people, their friends & families across the UK. They operate a freephone telephone helpline service as well as online chat and email support.

Hate crime

  • Stop Hate UK

    Stop Hate UK provide a LGBT hate crime support line and tools to report hate crime online.

Homelessness and housing

  • Shelter England

    Shelter are the UK housing and homelessness charity and can provide online, in person, and telephone helpline advice.

  • Shelter Scotland

    Shelter Scotland provide similar services in Scotland.

  • Shelter Cymru

    Shelter Cymru provide similar services in Wales.

  • Simon Community NI

    Simon Community NI provide homelessness support throughout Northern Ireland and have a 24/7 helpline.
